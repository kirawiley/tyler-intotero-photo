PORTRAITS

Tyler is a 23 year old self-taught photographer from Orange County, California. With his Sony a6000 and tripod, he utilizes long-exposure techniques for his beach and sunset shots. He also has a strong artistic eye for capturing models and is looking to expand his portraits portfolio. Soon Tyler will be traveling to Joshua Tree to photograph the Milky Way, a subject that he's been excited about for quite some time.

Besides photography, Tyler enjoys hockey. He is an Anaheim Ducks fan and plays on his own ice hockey team every week. He's also a frequent online gamer and a dog owner to a Havanese mix named Baxter.
